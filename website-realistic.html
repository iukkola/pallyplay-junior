What PallyPlay Does

A prediction gaming platform that uses football matches as opportunities for character development. Simple concept: combine match excitement with brief reflection moments.

Core Mechanism

Players make predictions during live matches. Rankings change in real-time with every goal, card, and event. After matches, players answer 2-3 reflection questions specific to their country's social challenges. No answers = no experience points.

Technical Features

  • Real-time betting with dynamic leaderboards
  • GPS detection for stadium attendance (no gaming advantages)
  • WhatsApp integration for friend group formation
  • Post-match reflection questions tailored to local issues

Why Young People Use It

Unlike traditional prediction apps, rankings change constantly during matches. No waiting 90 minutes for results.

Engagement Mechanics

  • Immediate feedback: Predict first goal at 25 minutes, goal scored at 12 minutes = ranking drops instantly
  • Social pressure: Friend notifications when positions change
  • Achievement system: Recognize sportsmanship, not just correct predictions
  • Stadium experiences: Connect with other fans when physically present

Example: Manchester derby. User leads predictions until minute 30 when Haaland scores. Drops to 4th place immediately. Friend overtakes. Notifications buzz. Heart rate stays elevated entire match.

Club and League Benefits

Football organizations get direct influence over their fanbase behavior through post-match questions and positive reinforcement systems.

For Individual Clubs

  • Direct communication channel with young supporters
  • Ability to reinforce club values during high-engagement moments
  • Stadium experience enhancement for attending fans
  • Measurable impact on supporter behavior and attitudes

For Leagues (Premier League, La Liga, etc.)

  • Unified platform for positive fan engagement across all clubs
  • Reduced toxicity in online football discussions
  • Educational content delivery during peak emotional investment
  • Data on fan behavior patterns and intervention effectiveness

Practical Implementation

Phase 1: Single League Pilot

  • Partner with one major European league
  • 5,000 users across 4 countries
  • Focus on Champions League matches for broad appeal
  • Measure engagement rates and behavioral change indicators

Phase 2: Multi-League Expansion

  • Add Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga partnerships
  • 50,000 active users
  • Club-specific content and messaging
  • Integration with club supporter databases

Business Model

Revenue sharing with partner leagues and clubs. Premium features for enhanced analytics. Educational licensing to schools and youth organizations.

Expected Outcomes

Measurable Changes (6-month timeline)

  • 20% reduction in toxic language in app-based football discussions
  • Increased cross-cultural appreciation among users
  • Higher rates of positive leadership behavior in peer groups
  • Improved conflict resolution skills during competitive situations

Long-term Impact (2-year timeline)

  • Cultural shift in how young Europeans engage with football
  • Reduced tribalism in supporter cultures
  • Stronger connection between football clubs and community values
  • Model replicable in other sports and regions

Reality Check: Not all users will engage with reflection questions. Success means 60-70% participation rates and measurable positive change in that group. Even partial adoption across 200M young Europeans represents significant social impact.
